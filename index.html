Our Story

Inspired by the bustling coffee culture of Ireland's capital, two coffee enthusiasts embarked on a quest to bring the finest coffee beans from across the globe to your doorstep.Our journey began with a love for travel and a quest for the perfect cup of coffee. After visiting the vibrant markets and bustling cafes of Morocco, we were captivated by the rich history and bold flavors of Moroccan coffee.Inspired by our experiences in this enchanting land, we embarked on a mission to bring the essence of Moroccan coffee culture to Dublin. Through countless visits to local coffee farms and immersive blending sessions, we honed our craft and perfected our unique blend. Every batch of coffee we import undergoes a meticulous selection process to ensure that only the finest beans make it to your cup. From the moment the beans are harvested to the final roast, we uphold the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
