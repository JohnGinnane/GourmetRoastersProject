Coffee Making Classes

@ Gourmet Roasters

Beginner

Our beginner class covers the basics of coffee brewing, including:

  • Introduction to coffee beans
  • Grinding and brewing techniques
  • Brewing methods (e.g., drip, French press)
  • Espresso Making
  • Milk Frothing

Price: €50

Advanced

Our advanced class builds upon the basics and dives deeper into:

  • Understanding coffee origins
  • Coffee art
  • Cupping and tasting
  • Espresso extraction
  • Roasting fundamentals

Price: €100
